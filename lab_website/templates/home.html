Welcome to 1210 Lab!

Welcome to Houqiang Li and Wengang Zhou's Lab homepage.

Our research group is in Lab of Multimedia Computing and Communication (MCC), University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). Research in our lab mainly focus on image/video coding, multimedia retrieval, video understanding, and computer vision. Especially, our research projects include image retrieval, action recognition, sign language recognition, tracking, etc.
